Paris Through my Lens

One of the most shaping experiences of my life was my study abroad in Paris, France. I was challenged to capture the city exactly as I experienced it. I shot this film on the first camera I ever got. It is a small blue Canon digital camera that I got in 2015 for my 5th grade trip. I also took it to Paris when I visited as a child. This film is not technically advanced, but makes me feel nostalgic, and truly captures a city beyond important to the cinema as a whole.
