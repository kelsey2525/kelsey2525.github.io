Super8 Film: Méliès Inspired Magic Show

This was what my group came up with when given our first ever roll of film to shoot with. My groupmates Jak and Alexandra were part of my study abroad program in Paris in the summer of 2024, and our journey there brought to our eyes the genius that was Georges Méliès. We wanted to use this roll of Super8 film to capture the magic that film truly is. We used bucket processing on reversal film and then used a laser scanner to digitize it. Very minimal digital editing. Enjoy this fun!
