Dance

I have been a dancer my whole life. I have been a hip-hop dancer since 2016, and I currently am the Director of a hip-hop dance group called VeRVE Collisionz. I am a choreographer, I manage funding, costumes, practices, guest instructors, and planning and executing performances. My group has a fall show, and hosts a spring Street Dance Competition involving hip-hop dance groups around the state of Colorado. I am incredibly passionate about the sport, art, and culture of dance, particularly hip-hop dance. About Me

I am a full-time Film Production student at the University of Colorado, Boulder and will graduate Spring 2026 with Latin Honors upon creating and defending an Honors Thesis that I am currently researching/creating/writing. I am additionally dually minoring in Communications and Creative Technology and Design. I am extremely excited for Sundance Film Festival to be coming to Boulder, CO, and at the prospect of potentially becoming a part of it. I am passionate about strengthening the creative community in Colorado.

☆

I am eager for roles regarding Creative Coordination, Production Design, and Production Assisting. I am openly willing to experiment with several roles in creative and communication-related realms. I am incredibly excited to gain industry experience in pre-production and production of movies, advertisements, television, arts, and other media.

☆

Summer of 2025, I was the videographer for the online course of two therapy professionals. I filmed and edited the course itself, 17 interviews with other professionals, multiple trailers, a keynote speech, and a speaker reel. This is hands-on independent video experience.

Summer of 2024, I interned for a local photographer and entrepreneur, helping with lighting and overall production of senior portraits for high school seniors.

I studied abroad in Paris, France during May and June of 2024, learning the historical significance of France in the context of Cinema, and gaining creative and practical understandings of the art of film.

☆

I am the Director of a Hip-Hop Dance group called VeRVE Collisionz on the CU Boulder campus. This role has provided me with strong skills in communication, leadership, funding, administration, and creative and organizational operations.

I currently work for the CU Boulder Honors College of Arts and Sciences, assisting various academic events, working with guest speakers, organizing logistics, managing emails, and creating an inclusive space for honors staff and students.

☆

I aspire to have a career in creative production in the future. For now, I will gain experience towards achieving that dream.

I am on time, organized, attentive, creative, communicative, and—most importantly—passionate.
