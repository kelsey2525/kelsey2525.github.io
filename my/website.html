Dance

I have been a dancer my whole life. I have been a hip-hop dancer since 2016, and I currently am the Director of a hip-hop dance group called VeRVE Collisionz. I am a choreographer, I manage funding, costumes, practices, guest instructors, and planning and executing performances. My group has a fall show, and hosts a spring Street Dance Competition involving hip-hop dance groups around the state of Colorado. I am incredibly passionate about the sport, art, and culture of dance, particularly hip-hop dance. To the right, are links to my group's YouTube and Instagram pages, where you can learn more about what we do, and even watch performances!
