Super8 Film: A SUPER GR8 WEEKEND

Memories of friendship, love, and nature captured on Super8 film. Shot over a weekend during my senior year of college in a special place with special people. This was only my second time ever working with the true film medium. I used bucket processing and Super8 reversal film for this project, and scanned it using a laser scanner. I did very minimal digital editing. I hope you enjoy this special preservation of memory.
