We'll Meet at the Top ~ A Romantic Narrative

My first Romantic Narrative. My capstone project for my undergraduate program. This film means a lot to me. At times, it is the project I am most proud of, and other times I can't even watch it because it took so long to create and I can't help but notice all the details that I am less fond of. The editing process was quite challenging due to using multiple softwares and being in the learning process with many aspects of it. I think this is something every artist can relate to. Regardless, I am so grateful for the team that came together to produce this film. I am grateful to be able to capture a story of love, in a world where love may be under or improperly valued and shared. Please enjoy the final version of "We'll Meet at the Top".
