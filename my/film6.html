Pumpkin Pie

Somewhat experimental, somewhat straightforward, this film is a synthesis of a film made by myself, and another film made my one of my classmates. The goal was to synthesize themes from both. Some of those themes are: relationships, friendship, horror, cooking, overwhelming emotions, and introspection. This was a really interesting exercise, and pushed me to explore a genre I am less familiar with.
